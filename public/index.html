ACADEMIC PROJECTS


Project 1:Bridge Monitoring System

A Bridge Monitoring System is an advanced framework designed project to ensure the structural health and safety of bridges. It utilizes a network of sensors to continuously measure critical parameters like stress, strain, vibration, and temperature. Data from these sensors is transmitted to central processing units, where software analyzes it in real-time to detect anomalies and potential issues. This continuous monitoring enables early detection of structural problems, allowing for timely maintenance and reducing the risk of unexpected failures.
